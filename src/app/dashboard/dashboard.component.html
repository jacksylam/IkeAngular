Xi - Interim moisture storage:
Ei - Total depth of water removed by ET from the plant-root zone during day
Mm - Moisture-storage capcity of plant root zone
DR - Daily rate of direct recharge
Qi - Ground water recharge during day {{DayValues[0].Qi}}
Mi - Moisture storage at the end of the current day {{DayValues[0].Mi}}

If Xi - Ei <=M m, then Qi=D R and Mi=X i - Ei

If Xi - Ei > Mm, then Qi = (Xi - Ei - Mm) + DR and Mi = Mm
